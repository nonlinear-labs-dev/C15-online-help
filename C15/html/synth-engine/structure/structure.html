5.2 Structure and Components

Signal Flow

The overall signal flow of the synthesis engine can be simplified as follows:

Branches A and B produce signals which are passed to the filter section. A mix from the branches and filters is defined and passed to the effect section and finally to the output. A second mix from the filters and effects is defined for the Feedback bus.
All components before the effects are polyphonic and single-channel. The effect section is monophonic and dual-channel (stereo).
There is a symmetric approach for the Oscillators, Shapers and Envelopes. Envelope A, Oscillator A and Shaper A are quasi-identical to Envelope B, Oscillator B and Shaper B. Envelope C is for modulation purposes.
For the main output, soft clipping is implemented (using a sine curve).
